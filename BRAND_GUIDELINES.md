# ALTIORA ESTATES Brand Guidelines

Position: a global luxury brand creating flight-access luxury estates.

Palette: obsidian `#070706`, ivory `#f3efe6`, champagne `#c8ad7f`, titanium `#8d918f`, muted stone `#b9b1a3`.

Typography: elegant editorial serif for major claims; restrained system sans for interface and technical language.

Visual language: cinematic remote Italian landscapes, stone, glass, titanium, warm timber, soft dawn or dusk light, no visible crowds, no exaggerated futurism.

Logo: uppercase wordmark with wide spacing and a minimal altitude/ascending trajectory symbol. Do not add wings, drones, cliché mountain icons or bright technology colors.
