A New Era

Personal flight will transform geography.

Roads have defined property values for centuries. Emerging personal electric aircraft may redefine accessibility. Remote mountain estates could become tomorrow's most desirable addresses: private, silent, elevated and no longer psychologically distant.

Italy's Hidden Treasure

One of Europe's great mountain archives.

Italy possesses one of Europe's largest concentrations of abandoned mountain heritage suitable for restoration, depending on local regulations: historic stone villages, alpine farmhouses, mountain refuges and ruins shaped by centuries of altitude.
